Guidelines

Our values and expectations for maintaining a collaborative space.

01
Respectful Communication

ForgeAlly is built on connection. Treat all peers and mentors with courtesy. Harassment or hate speech is strictly prohibited.

02
No Spam or Promotion

Do not spam channels or DMs with ads or links. Keep promotional material confined to designated lounge channels.

03
Collaboration and Sharing

We encourage helping others learn. Sharing files, notes, and mentoring help makes the community forge stronger goals.
